# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

Number Detective values your privacy. This project is a non-profit tool for education and brain exercise.

### 1.1 Local Data
The following data is stored only locally on your device:
- High scores
- Game statistics
- Personal settings (sound, language, etc.)

### 1.2 Third-Party Services
This application **does not use** any third-party tracking mechanisms (Analytics, etc.) or advertising networks (AdMob, etc.).

## 2. Data Security
All your data is stored securely on your device. The application does not send any personal data to our servers.

## 3. Advertisements
Our application is **ad-free.** it does not contain any advertising content that would distract or disrupt focus.

## 4. Children's Privacy
The application is safe for users of all ages and does not collect any data that would threaten children's privacy.

## 5. Contact
For questions or feedback, you can contact the developer.
